About Me

Hi there! My name is Ahilan Subbaian and I am a recent graduate of Stevens Institute of Technology, where I earned my Bachelor's and Master's degrees in Quantitative Finance and Machine Learning, respectively. I am highly skilled in programming languages such as Java, Python, and C++, and have experience using Python packages such as Pandas, Numpy, and TensorFlow. In my current role as a Software Engineer at Bloomberg LP, I am excited to continue learning and growing in my profession. I am particularly interested in expanding my skillset to include C++ programming, and am eager to learn more about this language and how it can be applied in my field.
